Your Voice Will Be Heard

CivicSays is a local government request and complaint tracking system that bridges the gap between residents and local officials. Residents can submit requests or complaints with photos and videos, receiving a unique tracking ID to monitor progress. Government officials can manage tickets, update statuses, and communicate with residents through comments, creating a transparent and efficient feedback system for community issues.
